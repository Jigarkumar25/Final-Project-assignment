Welcome to Empower Ability Labs!

Empower Ability Labs is a hub for learning and empathy building. We are on a mission to foster understanding and promote inclusive digital experiences for all. We offer a range of services designed to promote accessibility awareness, drive inclusivity, and enhance the user experience. We help you find answers on how people with disabilities use technology and navigate the digital world and what tools they employ.

Our approach

Empower Ability Labs utilizes a hands on approach to raise awareness and promote empathy. Our immersive workshops and lab days provide participants with a unique opportunity to step into the shoes of individuals with disabilities and navigate the digital world from their perspective.

Services

We offer dedicated programs that help teams build empathy and understand the challenges people with disabilities face when they use technology and move through different environments.

Empathy lab days and workshops

Our lab days and workshops give people a chance to try assistive technologies and complete real tasks that mirror the barriers people with disabilities face every day. These hands on sessions build awareness and spark practical ideas for change.

Inspirational speakers

Invite a speaker with disabilities to share their journey with your team. This powerful addition to your event offers insights that resonate inspire educate and deepen your teams understanding of inclusion.

Usability testing

Go beyond WCAG. Involve people with disabilities in usability testing to find accessibility issues early when fixes are more practical and cost effective. Our community includes people who use many types of assistive technology and have a wide range of technical skills from beginner to expert. They can support your product work from user research to design to quality checks.
